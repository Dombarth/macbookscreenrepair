LCD Only Replacement Eligibility

LCD only replacement is our preferred repair when the conditions are right. The team swap just the internal panel while keeping everything else original. Its faster and more affordable than full display replacement.

But LCD only has strict eligibility requirements. Not every MacBook qualifies. We check for these conditions when you drop off and wont proceed if the requirements arent met.

All conditions must be met:

  • No dents, bends or warping in the display frame
  • No signs of liquid exposure around the display
  • Screen bezel and corners in good physical condition
  • Housing structurally sound

If any of these conditions fail we cant do LCD only replacement. Theres no workaround. A new panel in a damaged housing wont seat properly and creates problems down the line. In those situations full display replacement is the option.

What LCD Only Preserves

When we do LCD only replacement your original display housing stays in place. This includes the metal frame, camera module, backlight layers and all the cabling in the lid. We just remove the failed panel and fit a new one.

When We Refuse

We turn away LCD only repairs that dont meet requirements. Frame damage, liquid exposure, cracked bezels, bent corners. These all mean the housing itself needs replacing not just the panel inside. We tell you this at assessment.

Assessment Process

Bring your MacBook in and the team check everything. We look at the frame condition, check for liquid indicators, assess the bezel and corners. Takes a few minutes. Then we can tell you whether LCD only is possible or if full replacement is needed.

Questions

Common Questions About MacBook Screen Repair

Answers to questions customers ask about LCD replacement, eligibility requirements and what to expect from the repair process.

Get In Touch
Whats the difference between LCD only and full display replacement?
LCD only replacement means we swap just the internal LCD panel while keeping your original display housing, backlight layers, camera and lid electronics. Full display replacement means the entire lid assembly gets replaced which is necessary when theres frame damage, dents, liquid exposure or structural issues.
How do I know if my MacBook qualifies for LCD only repair?
Your MacBook needs to meet all the eligibility requirements. No dents or bends in the display frame, no signs of liquid damage around the screen, and the bezels and corners need to be in good condition. We check this when you drop off. If theres any structural damage or liquid exposure we cant do LCD only.
Will I lose True Tone after a screen repair?
It depends on the repair type. LCD only replacement keeps your original display housing and electronics so True Tone usually stays working. Full display replacement can affect True Tone because Apple ties certain features to the original hardware calibration. Independent repairers cant override these Apple system controls.
Do you offer mail in or shipping repairs?
No we only do drop off repairs. You need to bring your MacBook to us for assessment and repair. We dont accept posted machines or do any remote service. This lets us properly assess the damage and discuss options with you in person.
What warranty do you offer on screen repairs?
We offer a 60 day limited warranty on internal LCD panel replacements. This covers the replaced component and our workmanship. It doesnt cover physical damage, liquid damage, impact damage or issues unrelated to the screen we replaced.
