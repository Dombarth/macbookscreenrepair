MacBook Display Specialists

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of MacBook display repairs. Thats what we do. Screen repairs only. Not general computer repairs, not phone repairs, not accessories. Just MacBook screens.

The team have been working on MacBook displays for years. Weve seen most issues that come through. Cracked screens from drops, dead panels from impact, flickering displays, lines running across the image, backlight problems. The common stuff and the unusual stuff.

We offer two main repair types. LCD only replacement where we swap just the internal panel while keeping your original housing. And full display assembly replacement for situations where LCD only isnt possible. We assess each machine and recommend whats appropriate.

Why Screen Repair Only

Focusing on one thing means doing it properly. General repair shops handle everything from phones to printers to game consoles. They cant specialise in any one thing. We chose to focus on MacBook screens because display repairs have specific requirements that benefit from dedicated attention.

LCD panel replacement requires proper technique and tooling. The panels are bonded to housings and separating them needs care. Housing assessment for eligibility matters. Getting the reassembly right matters. These things come from doing this work consistently not occasionally.

Honest Assessment

The team are upfront about what each MacBook needs. We dont push LCD only repairs when they wont work. We dont do full display replacement when LCD only is fine. We check each machine properly and tell you which repair type is appropriate.

Sometimes that means telling people we cant help them with LCD only even though thats what they wanted. If the housing is damaged it wont work properly. We explain why and give them options. No point doing a repair that wont last.

Drop Off Service

All repairs are drop off only. You bring the MacBook to us, we assess it, discuss options and give you pricing. This approach lets us properly inspect the damage. Photos dont show frame condition properly. Liquid exposure isnt always visible. Physical assessment catches things that remote evaluation misses.

The drop off approach also means we can have an actual conversation about what the MacBook needs. We explain the options, answer questions and make sure you understand what youre getting before any work starts.

Service Area

We service MacBook owners across Western Sydney, The Hills District and North West Sydney areas. Customers come from Parramatta, Castle Hill, Blacktown, Penrith, Baulkham Hills, Rouse Hill, Kellyville, Bella Vista, Seven Hills, Epping and all the surrounding suburbs.

The drop off only approach works well for the region. People can bring their MacBook in, get it assessed and pick it up once the work is done. No shipping delays, no mail in complications.

What We Dont Do

We dont do mail in or shipping repairs. No remote service. We need to see the MacBook in person to properly assess it.

We dont do non screen repairs. Logic board problems, battery issues, keyboard replacements, that sort of thing isnt what we do. We focus on displays only.

We dont sell accessories. Dont sell parts. Just repair services.

At MacBook Screen Repair we just like fixing displays. Thats what the team are good at. Thats what we focus on. If your MacBook screen is damaged bring it in and we can take a look.
