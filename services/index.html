MacBook Screen Repair Services

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of MacBook display repairs. The team offer two main repair types. LCD only replacement where we swap just the internal panel while keeping your original housing. And full display assembly replacement for situations where LCD only isnt possible.

LCD Only Replacement

Replace just the internal LCD panel while keeping your original display housing, camera, backlight and lid electronics intact. Available when the housing is undamaged with no dents, bends or liquid exposure.

Learn More

Full Display Replacement

Complete display assembly replacement including housing, panel, camera and all internal components. Required when there is frame damage, liquid exposure or the housing is compromised.

Learn More

True Tone Information

Understanding how Apple controls True Tone and what to expect after screen replacement. LCD only usually preserves it. Full display replacement can affect it.

How It Works

All repairs start with drop off assessment. You bring your MacBook in, we check the damage and housing condition, determine which repair type is needed and give you accurate pricing. No obligation until you approve the work.

Assessment Process

When you drop off we look at the screen damage, check the display frame for dents or bends, look for liquid damage indicators and assess the bezel and corners. This tells us whether LCD only is possible or if full display replacement is needed.

Assessment takes a few minutes. We explain what we find, what repair options are available and give you pricing. You decide whether to go ahead.

Drop Off Only

All repairs are drop off only. No shipping, no mail in service. You bring the MacBook to us, we assess it properly and discuss options in person. This approach lets us catch things that photos miss.
