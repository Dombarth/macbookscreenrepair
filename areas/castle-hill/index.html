LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is available when the display housing qualifies. We swap just the internal LCD panel and keep everything else intact. Your original metal frame, camera, backlight layers and lid electronics all stay in place. Its the more targeted repair option.

The eligibility requirements are strict though. No dents or bends in the frame. No signs of liquid exposure around the display. Bezel and corners undamaged. If theres frame damage of any kind LCD only wont work properly. We check all of this when you drop off.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be structurally sound with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or bezel damage. Learn more about LCD only repair.

Full Display Replacement

When LCD only isnt possible we offer full display assembly replacement. This is needed when theres frame damage, liquid has gotten into the display area, corners are bent or the housing is compromised in some way. The entire display assembly gets replaced.

Full display replacement includes the housing, LCD panel, camera module, backlight assembly and all cabling. Its a complete swap. More involved than LCD only and costs more because youre getting the full assembly not just one component. Note that True Tone may be affected by full display replacement. Check our pricing guide for indicative costs.

Common Screen Issues

MacBooks from Castle Hill come in with the usual types of damage. Cracked screens from drops. Sometimes the MacBook gets knocked off a desk or table. Sometimes something lands on it when its closed. The screen cracks but mechanically everything else is fine.

We also see dead displays where nothing shows on screen. Flickering panels that strobe or flash. Lines running across the display vertically or horizontally. Backlight problems where sections of the screen are dimmer than others. These issues are usually fixable with the right repair approach.

The specific repair needed depends on the housing condition. Undamaged housing means LCD only is usually possible. Damaged housing means full display replacement.
