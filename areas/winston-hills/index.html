LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we prefer when the housing is in good condition. The display housing on older MacBooks is often still pristine even when the panel inside has failed. In these cases we just swap the LCD panel and keep everything else.

The eligibility requirements are strict though. No frame damage. No dents or bends. No liquid exposure. The bezel needs to be intact. We check all of this when you drop off and determine whether LCD only is possible for your particular machine.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement becomes necessary when the housing itself is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural issues. In these situations we replace the entire display assembly. New housing, new panel, new camera, everything.

This costs more than LCD only but when the housing is compromised theres no other option. A new panel in a damaged housing causes problems. We explain the situation clearly and give you honest pricing so you can make an informed decision.

Older MacBook Models

Winston Hills has alot of older MacBook models still in daily use. The 2015 to 2019 MacBook Pro models are common. MacBook Air from that era too. These machines were built well and with proper care they last for years.

Screen failures on these older models are usually age related. The backlight goes dim, lines appear, or the panel just stops displaying. We can usually fix these with LCD only replacement assuming the housing is in good shape. Its a cost effective way to get more life out of a machine that still works well.
