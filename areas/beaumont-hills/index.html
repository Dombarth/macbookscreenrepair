MacBook Screen Repair for Beaumont Hills

Beaumont Hills is a newer suburb near Rouse Hill with modern housing developments. Young families moving in, nice streets, that new suburb feel. People settling into their homes and setting up their lives.

We see the newer MacBook models from this area including the M1 and M2 machines. Screen issues on these newer models are often hairline cracks that spread, or pressure damage from bags and accidents. The displays are good but theyre not indestructible.

We specialise in MacBook screens only. No phones, no tablets, no general repairs. Just display work for MacBook Air and MacBook Pro.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we do when the display housing is undamaged. We swap the internal LCD panel while keeping everything else. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight stays.

Requirements are strict. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure. Bezel intact. We assess at drop off.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage exists. Frame damage, bent corners, liquid exposure. We replace the complete assembly.
