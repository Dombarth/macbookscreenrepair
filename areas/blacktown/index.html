MacBook Screen Repair for Blacktown

At MacBook Screen Repair we service customers from Blacktown and the wider Western Sydney region. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement for all MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models. Drop off only with assessment before any work begins.

We specialise in MacBook displays only. Thats all we do. No general computer repairs, no phone repairs, no accessories. When you bring a MacBook in from Blacktown with screen damage we know exactly how to assess it and what repair options are available.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is our preferred repair when the housing allows for it. We swap the internal LCD panel while keeping your original display housing intact. Metal frame stays. Camera stays. Backlight layers stay. Just the panel gets replaced.

Not every MacBook qualifies though. The display housing needs to be structurally sound. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid damage around the display. Bezel and corners in good condition. We check all of this when you drop off and wont proceed with LCD only if the requirements arent met.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

When the housing is damaged full display replacement is the option. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure in the display area, cracked bezels. These situations mean we cant just swap the panel. The whole assembly needs to be replaced.

Full display replacement swaps everything. New housing, new panel, new camera module, new backlight assembly. Its more extensive than LCD only and costs accordingly. But sometimes its what the MacBook needs.

Screen Issues We See

MacBooks from Blacktown come in with all the usual screen problems. Cracked displays from drops are common. The MacBook falls off a desk or gets knocked around in transit. Sometimes the lid closes on something and the screen cracks from the pressure.

We also see dead screens that wont display anything. Flickering or strobing displays. Lines across the screen either horizontal or vertical. Backlight issues where parts of the screen are darker than others. All of these are repairable depending on the housing condition.
