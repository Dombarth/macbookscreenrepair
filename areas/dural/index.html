MacBook Screen Repair for Dural

Dural is a semi rural area on the edge of The Hills District. Hobby farms, rural properties, larger blocks. That country feel while still being close enough to the city. People out here value their space and tend to hold onto things longer.

We get MacBooks from hobby farms and rural properties as well as the more suburban areas of Dural. People out here tend to keep their machines longer so older models are common. The 2015 to 2019 MacBook Pro models still doing daily service.

We specialise in MacBook screens only. No phones, no tablets, no general repairs. Just display work for MacBook Air and MacBook Pro.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we do when the display housing is undamaged. We swap the internal LCD panel while keeping everything else. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight stays.

Requirements are strict. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure. Bezel intact. We assess all of this at drop off and confirm whether LCD only is possible.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage exists. Frame damage, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural issues. We replace the complete assembly.

Costs more than LCD only but when housing is damaged its the proper solution. We explain everything at assessment.

Older MacBook Models

Dural residents tend to keep their MacBooks longer. The machines get used until they really cant be used anymore. Screen failures on older models are usually age related. Backlight issues, panel failures, lines appearing.

We have good parts availability for older models. Worth getting a screen fixed on a machine that otherwise works fine rather than buying new.
