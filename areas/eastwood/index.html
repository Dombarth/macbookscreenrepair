MacBook Screen Repair for Eastwood

At MacBook Screen Repair we service customers from Eastwood and surrounding suburbs. LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement available for MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models. Drop off service with assessment before work begins.

Eastwood is well connected by train so we see MacBooks from right across the region. Epping, Ryde, Carlingford, West Ryde. People bring their machines in from all these areas when they need screen repairs.

We specialise in MacBook displays only. No phones, no general repairs. Just screens. When a damaged MacBook comes in from Eastwood we know what to look for.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping everything else intact. Metal frame stays. Camera stays. Backlight stays.

Eligibility requirements are strict. Housing must be undamaged. No frame dents. No liquid exposure. We assess at drop off.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement when housing is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure. Complete assembly gets replaced.
