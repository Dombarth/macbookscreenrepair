LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping your original housing. Metal frame, camera, backlight all stay in place. Just the damaged panel gets replaced.

Strict eligibility requirements apply. Housing must be undamaged. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure. We check this at drop off.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing is damaged. Frame damage, bent corners, liquid exposure. The complete assembly gets replaced including housing, panel and all components.
