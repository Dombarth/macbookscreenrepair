We specialise in MacBook screens only. No other devices, no other repairs. Just MacBook LCD panels and display assemblies.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is when we swap the internal display panel while keeping the aluminium housing intact. This works when the frame is undamaged.

We check the housing condition first. No dents, no bends, no liquid damage. If the housing is damaged, LCD only wont work.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when housing damage exists. We replace the complete display assembly with all components.
