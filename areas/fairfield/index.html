LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps the internal display panel while keeping your existing aluminium housing. This is the cheaper option when the frame is undamaged.

The housing must be in good condition. No dents, bends, or liquid exposure. We check this during assessment.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when the housing has physical damage. We replace everything including frame, screen, hinges and cables.
