LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping your original housing intact. The metal frame, camera module, backlight layers all stay in place. We just remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair type has strict eligibility. The housing needs to be structurally sound. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure around the display. Bezel intact. We check everything when you drop off and wont proceed if the requirements arent met.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be undamaged. No dents, bends, liquid exposure or structural issues. We assess this at drop off.

Full Display Replacement

When LCD only isnt possible we do full display replacement. This is needed when theres housing damage, frame dents, bent corners or liquid has gotten into the display assembly. The whole unit gets swapped out.

Full display replacement includes new housing, panel, camera and all internal components. More involved than LCD only and priced accordingly but sometimes its what the MacBook needs.

Common Issues

MacBooks from Penrith come in with the usual range of problems. Cracked screens from drops. Dead displays that wont show anything. Flickering panels. Lines across the screen. Backlight problems where parts are dimmer.

Most of these can be fixed with LCD only if the housing is undamaged. Housing damage means full display replacement is the only option.
