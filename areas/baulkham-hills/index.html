LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is available when the display housing qualifies. We swap the internal LCD panel while keeping your original housing intact. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight assembly stays. Just the panel gets replaced.

The eligibility requirements are strict. Housing needs to be undamaged. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure indicators. Bezel and corners in good condition. We assess all of this at drop off and wont proceed with LCD only if requirements arent met.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be structurally sound with no dents, bends or liquid exposure. We check this at assessment.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when the housing is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display, cracked bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly so the entire assembly gets replaced.

Full display includes new housing, panel, camera module and all internal components. Costs more than LCD only but sometimes its the only option depending on the damage.

Common Screen Issues

MacBooks from Baulkham Hills come in with typical screen problems. Cracked displays from drops and impacts. Dead screens that wont show anything. Flickering or strobing panels. Lines running across the display. Backlight issues.

The repair approach depends on housing condition. Undamaged housing usually means LCD only is possible. Damaged housing means full display replacement.
