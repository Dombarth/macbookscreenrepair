MacBook Screen Repair for Auburn

Auburn has a large population and plenty of small businesses. The commercial areas around the main streets are always busy. Shops, restaurants, services. Good community energy with people from all sorts of backgrounds making it work.

The team repair MacBooks from students at local schools as well as business owners in the commercial areas. Screen cracks from transport incidents are common. Crowded trains and buses, bags getting knocked around, the usual commuter problems.

We specialise in MacBook screens only. No phones, no iPads, no general computer repairs. Just display work for MacBook Air and MacBook Pro.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we do when the display housing is undamaged. We take out the internal LCD panel and put in a new one. Everything else stays in place. Metal housing, camera module, backlight layers.

Eligibility requirements are strict. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel intact. We assess all of this when you drop off and let you know if LCD only is possible.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage is present. Frame damage, bent corners, liquid in the display area, structural issues. We replace the complete assembly including everything.

This costs more than LCD only but damaged housing cant hold a new panel properly. Better to replace the whole thing and have it work right.

Student MacBooks

Alot of students from Auburn schools use MacBooks for their work. These machines get carried around all day, every day. In backpacks, on trains, between classes. Plenty of opportunities for screen damage.

We see cracked screens from bags being dropped, pressure damage from heavy books placed on top, accidental impacts in crowded spaces. Most of these can be fixed with LCD only if the housing is okay.
