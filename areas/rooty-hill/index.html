LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps the internal display panel while keeping your aluminium housing. This works when the frame is undamaged and is more economical.

The housing needs to be in good condition. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid damage. No warping.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has physical damage. We replace everything including frame, screen, hinges and internal cables.
