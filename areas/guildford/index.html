MacBook Screen Repair for Guildford

Guildford is an established suburb with good access to Western Sydney. Been around for ages with families and small business owners who have put down roots. The area has that working class feel where people look after what they have and fix things when they break.

We get MacBooks from families and small business owners in this area. The older MacBook Air models are still common here with screens that have started to fail after years of use. These machines were built well but nothing lasts forever.

We specialise in MacBook screens only. No phones, no general repairs, no other devices. Just MacBook display work. LCD only replacement when the housing is good and full display replacement when its not.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we do when the display housing is undamaged. We remove the internal LCD panel and fit a new one. Everything else stays. Metal frame, camera module, backlight assembly. Just the panel changes.

Eligibility requirements are strict. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure signs. Bezel intact. We assess all of this at drop off and determine whether LCD only is possible.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement becomes necessary when housing damage is present. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural issues. We replace the complete assembly including housing, panel, camera and all components.

This costs more than LCD only but when housing is compromised theres no other option. We explain everything clearly at assessment.

Older MacBook Air Models

The older MacBook Air models from 2017 to 2020 are still popular in Guildford. These machines are light, portable and handle everyday tasks fine. But after years of use the screens start having problems. Dim areas, lines appearing, panel failures.

Most of these can be fixed with LCD only replacement if the housing is undamaged. Good way to get more life out of a machine that still works well overall.
