We specialise in MacBook screens only. No phones, no tablets, no general computer work. Just MacBook display repairs for Air and Pro models.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is our preferred repair when conditions are right. We swap out the internal LCD panel and keep everything else. Metal housing stays, camera stays, backlight stays. Just the damaged panel gets replaced.

Requirements are strict though. The housing must be undamaged. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure. Bezel in good shape. We check all of this at assessment and confirm whether LCD only is possible for your machine.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage exists. Frame problems, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural issues. We replace the complete assembly with new housing, panel, camera and all components.

Costs more than LCD only but when housing is damaged its the only reliable option. We explain the situation clearly so you understand what repair is needed and why.
