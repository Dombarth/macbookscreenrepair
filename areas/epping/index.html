We specialise in MacBook displays only. No phones, no general repairs. Just MacBook screens. This focus means the team know exactly what to look for when a damaged machine comes in from Epping.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping your original housing. Metal frame stays. Camera stays. Backlight stays. Just the LCD panel gets replaced.

Eligibility requirements are strict. Housing must be undamaged. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure. Bezel intact. We check at drop off and wont proceed if requirements arent met.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure. The complete assembly gets replaced including housing, panel and all components.
