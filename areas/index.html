Our Repair Services

We offer two main repair types for MacBook screens. LCD only replacement is our preferred option when the display housing is undamaged. It replaces just the internal panel while keeping your original frame, camera and backlight. This typically costs less and preserves True Tone functionality.

Full display replacement is needed when theres frame damage, liquid exposure or housing issues. This replaces the complete lid assembly. Either way we assess your MacBook properly and explain all options before any work starts.
