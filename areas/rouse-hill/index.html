LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping your original display housing intact. Metal frame stays in place. Camera module stays. Backlight assembly stays. We just remove the damaged LCD and fit a replacement.

This repair has strict eligibility requirements. The display housing must be undamaged. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid exposure around the display. Bezel intact. We check all of this at drop off and wont do LCD only if conditions arent met.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be structurally sound with no dents, bends or liquid exposure. Assessed at drop off.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is the option when housing is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid in the display area, cracked bezels. These situations require the complete assembly to be replaced not just the panel.

Full display includes new housing, new panel, new camera module and all internal components. More extensive than LCD only and priced accordingly.

Common Issues

MacBooks from Rouse Hill come in with the usual problems. Cracked screens from drops. Sometimes kids knock them off tables. Sometimes they fall out of bags. Dead displays, flickering panels, lines across the screen. The standard range of issues.

Most can be fixed with LCD only if the housing is undamaged. Damaged housing means full display replacement is needed.
