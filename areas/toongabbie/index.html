LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is our preferred repair when conditions allow. We remove the damaged LCD panel and fit a new one. Everything else stays put. The metal housing, camera module, backlight layers. All of it remains in place.

Eligibility is strict though. The housing needs to be in good condition. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid exposure signs. Bezel intact without cracks. We check everything at assessment before confirming LCD only is possible.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage is present. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural problems. When these issues exist we replace the entire display assembly. New housing, new panel, new camera, the lot.

This approach costs more than LCD only because youre getting a complete assembly. But damaged housing cant hold a new panel properly. Better to replace the whole thing and have it work right.

Everyday Screen Damage

The MacBooks from Toongabbie come in with normal everyday damage. Cracked screens from accidental drops. Pressure damage from things placed on top of closed laptops. Lines appearing on the display over time. Backlight problems where the screen dims in patches.

Most of these issues can be fixed with LCD only replacement assuming the housing is undamaged. We assess each machine individually and determine the right repair approach.

Toongabbie MacBook Screen Repair Questions

Do you service MacBooks from Toongabbie?
Yes we service MacBook owners from Toongabbie and all of Western Sydney. Drop off your MacBook for assessment and we can check the screen damage.
How long does a screen repair take?
Most LCD only repairs are same day if we have parts in stock for your model. Full display replacement can take longer depending on parts availability. We give you a timeframe at assessment.
Can you repair my MacBook if the frame is dented?
Frame damage means LCD only replacement isnt possible. Full display replacement is the alternative which replaces the entire assembly including the housing. We explain options and pricing at assessment.
What warranty do you offer?
We provide a 60 day limited warranty on LCD panel replacements. This covers the replaced component and our workmanship. Physical damage and liquid damage occurring after repair are not covered.
