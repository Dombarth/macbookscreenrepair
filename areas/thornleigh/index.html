Thornleigh sits along the main northern railway line between Pennant Hills and Normanhurst. The Bunnings is a local landmark. Lots of bushland nearby, good schools in the area. Its got that quiet north shore feel while still being accessible.

Residents in Thornleigh include families and professionals who commute to the city. MacBooks are common for remote work and study. When screens break it disrupts the daily routine.

We specialise in MacBook screens only. No other devices. Just MacBook LCD panels and display assemblies.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps the internal display panel while keeping the aluminium housing. This is the cheaper option when there's no housing damage.

The housing must be in good condition. No dents or bends. No liquid damage. No warping of the frame.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when the housing has physical damage. We replace everything including frame, screen, hinges and internal cables.
