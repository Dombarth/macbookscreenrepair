LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is what we do when the display housing is undamaged. We take out the internal LCD panel and fit a new one. Everything else stays in place. Metal frame, camera module, backlight assembly. Just the panel changes.

The requirements are strict. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid exposure signs. Bezel intact. We assess all of this at drop off and let you know if LCD only is an option for your machine.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when housing damage is present. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid around the display, structural problems. We replace the complete assembly including housing, panel, camera and all components.

Costs more than LCD only because youre getting a full assembly. But when housing is damaged theres no other reliable option. We explain the situation and give you honest pricing.

Healthcare Worker MacBooks

MacBooks used by healthcare workers have their own patterns. Heavy daily use in demanding environments. Shift work means the machine is being carried at odd hours. Hospital cafeterias, break rooms, transport. Plenty of opportunities for accidents.

Screen damage from drops during busy shifts. Pressure cracks from bags getting bumped. Display failures from years of intensive use. We see it all from the Westmead area. Most can be fixed with LCD only if the housing is okay.
