LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is the option when the display housing is undamaged. We swap the internal LCD panel and keep the aluminium frame and other components.

The housing needs to be in good condition. No dents, bends, or liquid exposure. We check this during assessment.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is needed when the housing has physical damage. We replace the entire assembly including frame, screen, hinges and cables.
