MacBook Screen Repair for Wentworthville

Wentworthville has good train connections making it easy for people to drop off MacBooks on their way to work. The station is right there and the suburb has that commuter feel. People heading into the city for work, heading home in the evening. The train line runs right through.

We get a steady flow of repairs from this suburb. Alot of the 13 inch MacBook Pro models come through. These are the workhorses that people carry every day. Light enough to commute with but powerful enough for real work. And when the screen cracks it becomes urgent.

We focus exclusively on MacBook screens. No phones, no tablets, no general computer repairs. Just MacBook display work. LCD only replacement when possible and full display replacement when needed.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is our preferred approach when the display housing is undamaged. We remove the internal LCD panel and replace it while keeping everything else. The metal frame stays, the camera module stays, the backlight stays. Just the damaged panel gets swapped.

The requirements are strict. No frame dents or bends. No liquid exposure around the display. Bezel in good condition. We assess all of this when you drop off and determine whether LCD only is viable for your machine.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement becomes necessary when housing damage exists. Frame damage, bent corners, liquid exposure, structural problems. In these cases we replace the complete display assembly including housing, panel, camera and all components.

It costs more than LCD only but when housing is compromised there isnt another option. A new panel wont work properly in a damaged housing. We explain all of this at assessment.

MacBook Pro 13 Inch

The 13 inch MacBook Pro is the most common screen replacement we see from Wentworthville. These machines are everywhere. The 2016 to 2020 Touch Bar models. The M1 and M2 versions. They get used hard and eventually screens fail.

Screen cracks from transport incidents. Pressure damage from bags getting squeezed. Lines developing on the display over time. We handle all of it. Most 13 inch repairs can be done with LCD only if the housing is undamaged.
