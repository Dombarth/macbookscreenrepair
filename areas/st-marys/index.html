LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is the more affordable option when the aluminium housing is undamaged. We swap the internal LCD panel and keep everything else.

This requires the housing to be perfect. No dents, no bends, no liquid damage. If there's any frame damage, LCD only wont work.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when housing damage exists. We replace the complete display assembly including frame, screen, hinges and cables.
