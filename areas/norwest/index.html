LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement is our preferred repair method when the conditions are right. We remove just the internal LCD panel and replace it with a new one. Everything else stays in place. The metal housing, the camera module, the backlight assembly, all of it remains.

This approach has strict eligibility requirements though. The display housing needs to be in good condition. No dents or bends in the frame. No signs of liquid exposure around the display area. The bezel cant be cracked or chipped. We check all of this at assessment and wont do LCD only if the housing doesnt meet requirements.

LCD Only Eligibility: Display housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends, liquid exposure or frame warping. Assessment determines eligibility.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is the alternative when LCD only isnt possible. If theres frame damage, bent corners, liquid exposure signs, or structural issues with the housing then we need to replace the whole assembly. The complete display unit including housing, panel, camera and all internal components.

This costs more than LCD only because youre getting the complete assembly. But when the housing is damaged theres no point putting a new panel in there. It wont seal properly and youll have problems down the line. We explain all of this at assessment so you understand the options.

Common Screen Issues

MacBooks from Norwest come in with the usual range of problems. Cracked screens from desk accidents. The machine gets knocked by a coffee cup or falls off a laptop stand. Sometimes it happens in transit when people are rushing between offices.

Dead panels where the screen wont display anything at all. Flickering screens that make it impossible to work. Lines running across the display either vertically or horizontally. Backlight problems where part of the screen is dimmer than the rest. We see all of it from the business park.
