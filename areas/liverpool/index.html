MacBook Screen Repair for Liverpool

At MacBook Screen Repair we service customers from Liverpool and South West Sydney. LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement available for MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models. Drop off service with assessment before work begins.

Liverpool is a major centre in South West Sydney. We see MacBooks from across the area. Fairfield, Bankstown, Cabramatta, Prestons. People bring their MacBooks in from all across the Liverpool region when they need screen repairs.

The team specialise in MacBook displays only. No phones, no general repairs. Just screens. When a damaged MacBook comes in from Liverpool we know exactly what to check.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel while keeping everything else. Metal frame stays. Camera stays. Backlight stays. Just the damaged LCD gets replaced.

Eligibility requirements are strict. Housing must be undamaged. No frame dents. No liquid exposure. We assess at drop off.

LCD Only Eligibility: Housing must be undamaged with no dents, bends or liquid exposure.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement when housing is damaged. Frame dents, bent corners, liquid exposure. Complete assembly gets replaced.
