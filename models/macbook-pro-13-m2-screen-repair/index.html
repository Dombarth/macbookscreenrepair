MacBook Pro 13" M2 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Pro 13 inch M2 released in 2022. This is model identifier A2689 and was the last MacBook Pro with the Touch Bar design. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 13 inch MacBook Pro with M2 chip kept the older design with Touch Bar rather than switching to the notch design of the 14 and 16 inch models. 13.3 inch Retina display at 2560 x 1600 resolution. Apple discontinued this model after the M2 generation.

Last Touch Bar MacBook Pro

The 2022 M2 13 inch was the final MacBook Pro with Touch Bar. Same physical design as the M1 13 inch before it. Standard Retina display without the mini-LED backlighting or ProMotion of the larger Pro models. But still a capable machine that people use daily.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cabling. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. Full display replacement swaps the whole assembly including housing.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original housing. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight and cabling stays. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team assess this at drop off.

LCD only usually preserves True Tone because the original electronics remain in place.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. As such its a bigger repair. True Tone may not work after because Apple ties this to the original display.

Common Issues

We see the usual range of screen problems on the 13 inch M2 Pro. Cracked screens from drops, dead displays, flickering, backlight issues. The 13 inch form factor means these get carried around alot which leads to accidents.
