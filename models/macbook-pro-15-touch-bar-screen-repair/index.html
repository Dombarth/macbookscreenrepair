MacBook Pro 15" Touch Bar Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch Touch Bar models from 2016 to 2019. The A1707 covers 2016-2017 and A1990 covers 2018-2019. Both use a Retina display at 2880 x 1800 with P3 wide colour. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 15 inch Touch Bar Pro was the main workhorse for professionals for several years. Weve seen alot of them. Had one from Castle Hill where a graphic designer cracked the screen in a bag drop. Housing was fine so LCD only repair sorted it out and saved them compared to full assembly.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

These 15 inch models have a solid build. We had one where the corner got bent from a fall. Full assembly was the only option but it came out looking like new.

True Tone

True Tone is available on the 2018 and 2019 models (A1990) but not on 2016-2017 (A1707). On models that have it the calibration data lives in the display housing. As such LCD only repair keeps True Tone working. Full assembly means new housing and True Tone data doesnt transfer.

The team check which model you have and explain what applies to your machine before any work starts.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The Retina display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage is another common one where something gets pressed against the closed lid. The LCD can fail in sections.

The Touch Bar is a separate component. If thats working fine and only the main display is damaged we just do the screen.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" Touch Bar screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
