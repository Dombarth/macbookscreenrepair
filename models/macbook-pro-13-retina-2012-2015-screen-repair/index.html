MacBook Pro 13" Retina 2012-2015 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 13 inch Retina from 2012 to 2015. The A1425 covers late 2012 to early 2013 and A1502 covers late 2013 through early 2015. Both use a Retina display at 2560 x 1600. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 13 inch Retina Pro was a popular machine for years. Compact with a sharp screen. Weve seen alot of them. Had one from Baulkham Hills where the owner still used it for writing and web work. Screen cracked but housing was fine so LCD only repair made sense.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

On machines this age we see more housing wear. Dents from years of use, worn hinges. We check everything and explain your options.

No True Tone

The A1425 and A1502 predate True Tone so theres no calibration to preserve during repair. Makes things simpler in that regard. LCD only replacement is straightforward when the housing allows.

Parts for these older Retina displays are still available. Worth repairing if the machine works for you.

Model Differences

A1425 is the late 2012 and early 2013 models. A1502 is late 2013, mid 2014, and early 2015. Display repair is similar across all of them. The team figure out which you have from the model number on the bottom of the machine.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. Pressure damage where something pressed against the closed lid. On older machines we also see backlight issues, vertical lines, and display cables wearing out from years of opening and closing.

Some of these machines are over ten years old now. Age related problems are expected.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 13" Retina 2012-2015 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
