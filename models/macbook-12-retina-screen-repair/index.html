MacBook 12" Retina Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook 12 inch Retina from 2015 to 2017. The A1534 was Apples ultra portable MacBook with a Retina display at 2304 x 1440. Single USB-C port design. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 12 inch MacBook was all about portability. Thin and light for travel. Apple discontinued it but people still use them. Had one from Epping where the owner used it as their travel machine. Screen cracked after an airport incident. Housing was ok so LCD only repair got it back in service.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

The 12 inch MacBook is very thin. The display housing is delicate. We had one where a fall dented the corner. Full assembly was the only option but the repair came out looking good.

No True Tone

The A1534 doesnt have True Tone so theres no calibration to preserve during repair. Makes things simpler in that regard. LCD only replacement is straightforward when the housing allows.

Parts for the 12 inch MacBook are available. Worth repairing if you like the ultra portable form factor.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. The thin design means less structural protection. Pressure damage where something pressed against the closed lid. The LCD can fail in sections with dead areas.

These machines are lightweight so people tend to throw them in bags without much padding. Leads to damage over time.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook 12" Retina screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
