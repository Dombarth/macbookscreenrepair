MacBook Pro 13" 2016 (A1708) Display Overview

The late 2016 MacBook Pro 13" function key model brought the thin new design without Touch Bar. Model identifier A1708 covers this entry level variant featuring two Thunderbolt 3 ports and traditional function keys instead of the Touch Bar. This configuration appealed to users wanting the redesigned chassis at lower cost without Touch Bar complexity. The machine introduced butterfly keyboard and USB-C only connectivity to the entry tier.

Mac Screen Repair Sydney specialise in the aspects of Retina display repair for A1708 MacBook Pro models. The 13" display delivers 2560 x 1600 resolution with P3 colour support. We've worked on alot of these first generation function key models over the years. Quality replacement parts ensure proper display performance after repair.

Screen Repair Methods

Two repair approaches address screen damage on the 13" 2016 function key model. LCD only replacement removes the damaged internal panel while preserving your original aluminium housing and camera assembly. This requires housing without structural damage. Full display assembly replacement swaps the entire top section when housing dents or bends make LCD only unsuitable.

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess devices before recommending either path. We dont push assembly replacements when LCD only achieves equivalent results. Housing condition and damage type determine our suggestion. You get appropriate repair for your situation.

Display Technical Specifications

The A1708 features a 13.3-inch LED-backlit IPS Retina display at 2560 x 1600 native resolution. This provides 227 pixels per inch for sharp text and detailed images. The panel supports P3 wide colour gamut for accurate colour reproduction. Maximum brightness reaches 500 nits which handles typical environments adequately.

Display panel specifications match the Touch Bar A1706 model identically. Both configurations deliver the same screen quality. The A1708 simply lacks Touch Bar and has two ports versus four. Replacement panels meet original P3 and brightness specifications.

No True Tone Feature

The 2016 13" function key model does not include True Tone technology. This feature came with 2018 models. For repair purposes, this simplifies considerations since theres no True Tone data involved. Both LCD only and full assembly replacement deliver identical display functionality. No features lost regardless of repair method.

The display provides excellent P3 colour coverage for creative and general use. Absence of True Tone simply means no automatic white balance adjustment based on ambient lighting.

Flexgate Susceptibility

The 2016 13" models including A1708 can develop display cable wear over time. Apple routed the display cable through the hinge where repeated opening creates stress. Symptoms include stage lighting at the bottom edge, flickering, or display blackouts at certain angles. The issue progresses if unaddressed.

Apple extended warranty coverage for some units though many machines fall outside that program now. The team have repaired flexgate on A1708 models. Full display assembly replacement addresses cable problems definitively with new cables. We diagnose cable versus panel issues accurately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the A1708 screen same as A1706 Touch Bar?

Panel specifications are identical but the display assemblies are different parts. Housing differences mean they arent directly interchangeable though both deliver identical display quality.

Can this model get flexgate?

Yes, the A1708 uses similar display cable routing through the hinge as Touch Bar models. Flexgate symptoms can develop from repeated opening and closing over time.

Is this model worth repairing?

Depends on your needs and overall device condition. If the machine serves your purposes and works well otherwise, screen repair is more economical than replacement. We assess honestly.

Is the 2016 the same as 2017?

Both years share the A1708 identifier with identical displays. The 2017 has newer processors but screen parts work interchangeably between these model years.
