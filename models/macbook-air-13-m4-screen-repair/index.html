MacBook Air 13" M4 Screen Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Air 13 inch M4. The A3200 model uses a Liquid Retina display running at 2560 x 1664 resolution with True Tone. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what is required for your specific situation.

Had an A3200 come in from a customer over in Parramatta with a cracked screen from a drop. The housing was in good shape so we did LCD only which saved them alot compared to full assembly. Took about three hours and they had it back same day. Thats the kind of turnaround we aim for on these newer MacBook Air models.

LCD Only vs Full Display

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every MacBook Air before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement involves removing the cracked panel while keeping the original aluminium housing and camera assembly. The housing has to be free of dents and bends for this to work. If theres structural damage to the housing then full display assembly is the way to go.

Most of the M4 MacBook Air screens we see are candidates for LCD only. The machines are new enough that housings are usually still in good condition. We had one come in where someone had sat on it and bent the whole lid. Full assembly was the only option there. Cost more but the display works perfectly now.

True Tone on the M4

True Tone is the feature that adjusts white balance based on ambient lighting. On the A3200 the True Tone data lives in the original display housing. As such LCD only repair preserves True Tone because the housing stays in place. Full assembly replacement means new hardware and the True Tone calibration doesnt transfer across automatically.

The team have found most people dont notice True Tone missing after full assembly replacement. Its one of those features that works in the background. But if its important to your workflow we can discuss the options before proceeding with anything.

Common Issues We See

Cracked screens from drops are the most common repair on MacBook Air M4 models. Impact damage creates visible fracture patterns across the display. Pressure damage is another one we see alot where something heavy has rested on the closed MacBook in a bag. The LCD fails in patches without visible cracks on the outside.

Some units come in with display cable issues. Flickering or intermittent blackouts. The M4 Air is still new so were not seeing wear related cable failures yet but physical damage can affect the cable connection.

Pricing and Turnaround

MacBook Air 13" M4 screen repair pricing varies with the type of repair needed. LCD only runs cheaper than full assembly when housing condition permits. We quote after looking at the machine since photos dont always show housing damage accurately. The assessment is free with no obligation to go ahead.

For LCD only repairs on A3200 models we can usually turn around same day if parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss timeline when you get in touch.
