MacBook Air 13" M4 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we service the MacBook Air 13 inch M4 released in 2025. This is model identifier A3200. The team handle LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition your MacBook Air is in when it comes through.

The 2025 MacBook Air with M4 chip has the same 13.6 inch Liquid Retina display as the M3 generation. 2560 x 1664 resolution with P3 wide colour and True Tone. When the screen gets cracked or damaged we can replace either just the LCD panel or the complete display assembly depending on what the damage looks like.

About the Display

The MacBook Air 13" M4 uses the notch design that Apple introduced with the M2 generation Air. Slim bezels around the display with the camera sitting in a notch at the top. Display supports 500 nits brightness and has the same panel type as the M3 model before it.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight layers, camera module and all the cabling that connects to the logic board. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel and keep everything else. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the MacBook Air M4 means swapping just the internal LCD panel while keeping your original display housing intact. The metal frame stays, camera module stays, backlight assembly and all the cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair type is available when the display housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents or bends in the frame. No signs of liquid exposure around the display. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team check all of this at assessment before quoting.

LCD only is our preferred repair when conditions allow for it. Its more targeted than full display replacement. Keeps your original housing and camera. Usually preserves True Tone functionality since the original electronics stay in place.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement for the MacBook Air M4 is necessary when the housing itself has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display, cracked bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly and full replacement is the way to go.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD panel, new camera, new backlight, new cables. Everything. This is a bigger repair than LCD only but sometimes its what the MacBook needs to get it working properly again.

Full display replacement can affect True Tone. Apple ties this feature to the original display through firmware pairing. The replacement assembly doesnt have this pairing and independent repairers cant restore it. The display works fine for everything else but True Tone may not be available after.

Common Screen Issues

We see the usual range of screen problems on MacBook Air M4 models. Cracked screens from drops are common. The slim design means theres not alot of protection around the display if it takes an impact. Sometimes the MacBook gets knocked off a desk or dropped getting out of a bag.

Dead displays where the screen wont show anything. Flickering screens and lines running across the display. Backlight issues where parts are brighter or dimmer than they should be. These are all repairable with the right approach depending on what the housing looks like.

Identifying Your Model

The MacBook Air 13" M4 is model A3200. You can check your model by clicking the Apple menu and selecting About This Mac. It shows the model name and year. The model identifier is also printed on the bottom of the machine near the regulatory markings if you flip it over.

If your not sure what model you have bring it in and we can check. The team are familiar with all the MacBook Air models and can identify which one you have at assessment.

MacBook Air M4 Screen Repair Questions

Can you repair a cracked MacBook Air M4 screen?
Yes we repair cracked screens on the MacBook Air 13" M4. Either LCD only replacement if the housing is undamaged or full display replacement if theres frame damage. We determine which is needed at assessment.
Will True Tone still work after repair?
LCD only replacement usually preserves True Tone because the original housing and electronics stay in place. Full display replacement can affect True Tone because Apple ties this feature to the original display.
How long does the repair take?
Most LCD only repairs for MacBook Air M4 are same day if we have parts in stock. Full display replacement may take longer depending on parts availability. We give you a timeframe at assessment.
Do you need to keep the MacBook overnight?
Depends on the repair and parts availability. Some repairs are same day, others need longer. We let you know when you drop off.
