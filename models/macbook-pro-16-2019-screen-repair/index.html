MacBook Pro 16" 2019 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 16 inch 2019. The A2141 was the first 16 inch MacBook Pro and uses a Retina display at 3072 x 1920 with True Tone and P3 wide colour. This was the last big screen Intel Pro before Apple Silicon. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 16 inch Intel Pro was a popular machine for professionals. Big screen with serious power. Weve seen alot of them over the years. Had one from Penrith where a music producer dropped it at a gig. Cracked the display but housing was ok. LCD only repair saved them alot compared to full assembly.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

The 16 inch display is large with more surface area to potentially damage. We had one where the corner got bent from a fall. Full assembly was the only option but it came out looking brand new.

True Tone

True Tone adjusts display colour based on ambient lighting. On the A2141 the calibration data lives in the display housing. As such LCD only repair keeps True Tone working because that housing stays in place. Full assembly means new housing and True Tone data doesnt transfer across.

The team have explained this to alot of customers. Most find True Tone subtle in daily use but we make sure you understand before any work starts.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The Retina display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage is another one where something gets pressed against the closed lid. The LCD can fail in sections.

The Touch Bar above the keyboard is a separate component. If thats working fine and only the main display is damaged we just do the screen.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 16" 2019 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
