MacBook Air 2015-2016 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2015 and 2016 models. Both use model identifier A1466 with the same display assembly. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2015 and 2016 MacBook Air models use the classic Air design with MagSafe charging and the standard definition display at 1440 x 900 resolution. Not a Retina display but these machines are still being used and still need repairs when screens get damaged.

About the A1466

The A1466 model number covers the 2012 through 2017 MacBook Air. The display panels are compatible between years. If you have one of these with a damaged screen we can help regardless of which specific year it is.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly including housing gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing. Housing stays, camera stays, backlight stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel intact. The team at MacBook Screen Repair check all of this at assessment.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure, cracked bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats what these older machines need.

Common Issues

The 2015 and 2016 MacBook Airs have been around for quite a while now so weve seen alot of them with screen problems. Cracked screens, dead displays, flickering, backlight issues. These machines are getting old but people still use them.

Being older the housings have taken wear over the years. This can affect whether LCD only is possible. We assess each one when it comes in.

All MacBook Air 2015-2016 screen repairs are drop off only. Bring the MacBook in, we assess the damage and housing condition, work out whether LCD only or full display replacement is needed and give you pricing.
