MacBook Pro 13" 2016 Touch Bar Display Overview

The late 2016 MacBook Pro 13" with Touch Bar represented a significant redesign of Apple's professional notebook. Model identifier A1706 marks the first generation Touch Bar equipped 13" MacBook Pro. This release introduced the thinner chassis, Touch Bar interface, four Thunderbolt 3 ports replacing all legacy connections, and the butterfly keyboard. The design generated both excitement and controversy among users accustomed to previous generations.

Mac Screen Repair Sydney specialise in the aspects of Retina display repair for A1706 first generation MacBook Pro models. The 13" display delivers 2560 x 1600 resolution with P3 colour gamut. We've worked on alot of these original Touch Bar units since they launched. Understanding the design quirks and common issues helps us deliver reliable repairs.

Repair Options Available

Two repair paths address screen damage on 13" 2016 Touch Bar models. LCD only replacement extracts the damaged internal panel while keeping your original aluminium housing intact. This requires housing free from structural damage. Full display assembly replacement swaps the complete top section when housing dents or bends prevent LCD only work.

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney evaluate your device before recommending either option. We dont default to expensive assembly replacements when LCD only achieves equivalent results. Housing condition and damage pattern determine our suggestion.

Display Specifications

The A1706 features a 13.3-inch LED-backlit IPS Retina display at 2560 x 1600 native resolution. This delivers 227 pixels per inch for sharp text and detailed imagery. The panel supports P3 wide colour gamut for accurate colour reproduction in creative work. Maximum brightness reaches 500 nits which suits most lighting environments.

The 2016 Touch Bar 13" does not include True Tone technology. This feature arrived with the 2018 refresh. For repairs, this simplifies matters since theres no True Tone data to preserve. Both repair methods deliver identical display functionality.

Flexgate - A Known Issue

The 2016 Touch Bar MacBook Pro models became known for display cable failures dubbed flexgate. Apple designed the display cable to run through the hinge area where repeated opening and closing creates wear. Over time, this wear causes backlight failure manifesting as a bright band at the bottom of the screen. As damage progresses, display may fail completely at certain angles or altogether.

Apple acknowledged this problem and extended warranty coverage for affected units. Many machines now fall outside that program due to age. The team have repaired numerous flexgate cases on A1706 models. Full display assembly replacement addresses the issue definitively since it includes new cables.

Common Display Damage

Beyond flexgate, physical damage causes many repairs. Impact damage from drops creates cracked glass and LCD fractures. Pressure damage from bags and backpacks causes panel failures without visible external damage. The thin display assembly in this redesign offers less protection than older thicker MacBooks. Objects pressing on closed laptops cause alot of the pressure damage we see.

We diagnose damage type accurately since this determines repair approach. Physical damage shows visible patterns while cable issues present as backlight or intermittent failures without obvious physical cause.

First Generation Considerations

As the first Touch Bar generation, the 2016 models have the longest history of potential issues. Display cable design, butterfly keyboard mechanism, and thermal management all received refinements in later years. Some users experience multiple concerns simultaneously. We focus specifically on screen repair but can discuss overall device condition during assessment.

Screen repair makes sense when the rest of the machine works properly. If multiple components need attention, replacement might warrant consideration depending on your situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the 2016 display same as 2017?

Yes, both 2016 and 2017 Touch Bar 13" models share the A1706 identifier and use identical display assemblies. Repair parts work interchangeably between years.

What exactly is flexgate?

Flexgate describes display cable failures caused by cable wear at the hinge. Symptoms start as stage lighting (bright band at bottom) and progress to complete display failure. Its a design flaw in 2016-2017 models.

Is this model worth repairing?

Depends on overall condition and your needs. If the machine works well otherwise and suits your tasks, screen repair extends useful life economically. We provide honest assessment during evaluation.

Can LCD only repair fix flexgate?

LCD only replaces the panel but keeps original cables. For flexgate cable issues, full assembly replacement provides the definitive solution with new cables included.
