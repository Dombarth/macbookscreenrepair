MacBook Air 11" 2015 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Air 11 inch 2015. The A1465 was the last 11 inch Air that Apple made and uses a display at 1366 x 768. Compact little machine that was popular for its portability. We do LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 11 inch Air was discontinued after 2015 but weve still seen alot of them. People kept using them for travel and light work. Had one come in from Eastwood where the owner used it as their carry everywhere machine. Screen cracked after a fall from a cafe table. Housing was fine so LCD only repair got it sorted.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or warping for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly is required.

The 11 inch form factor means smaller display housing. We had one where the corner got bent badly from a drop. Full assembly was the only option but the repair came out looking good as new.

The 11 inch Display

The A1465 uses a non-Retina LCD panel at 1366 x 768 resolution. Smaller screen than the 13 inch models but worked well for the compact size. No True Tone on these machines so theres nothing extra to worry about during repair.

Parts for the 11 inch Air are still available. Worth repairing if the machine suits your needs.

Common Issues

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage is another common one where something presses against the closed lid. The LCD fails in sections but the glass can look fine from outside.

On machines this age we also see wear related problems. Backlight issues, vertical lines, display cables wearing out from years of use. All fixable.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Air 11" 2015 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no pressure.

Same day for LCD only when parts are available. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on stock. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
