MacBook Air 11" 2015 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 11 inch 2015 model. This is model identifier A1465 and was the last 11 inch MacBook Air that Apple made. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 11 inch MacBook Air was popular for its compact size and light weight. Apple discontinued the 11 inch in 2016 to focus on the 13 inch Air and 12 inch MacBook. If you still have one and the screen needs repair we can help.

Last 11 inch Air

The 2015 11 inch Air was the final version. Same basic design as the previous years but with updated internals. 11.6 inch display running at 1366 x 768 resolution. Compact machine that was great for travel but Apple eventually moved away from this size.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing. Housing stays, camera stays, backlight stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends. No liquid exposure. The team at MacBook Screen Repair check the housing condition at assessment.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly and full replacement is needed.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats whats needed.

Common Issues

The 11 inch Air has been discontinued for years now so these are all older machines. Weve seen cracked screens, dead displays, backlight issues, flickering. The compact size meant they got carried around alot which led to more drops over time.

Given the age the housings have often taken wear. This can affect LCD only eligibility. We assess each one individually.

Drop Off Service

All MacBook Air 11" screen repairs are drop off only. Bring it in, we assess the damage and housing, determine the repair type and give you pricing.
