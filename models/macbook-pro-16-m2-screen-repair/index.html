MacBook Pro 16" M2 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 16 inch M2. The A2780 uses a Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion at 3456 x 2234 resolution. Available with M2 Pro or M2 Max chips. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 16 inch M2 Pro is the big screen option for serious work. Video editing, 3D work, music production. Had one come in from Liverpool where the owner dropped it moving studios. Cracked the display pretty bad but the housing was still ok. LCD only repair saved them alot compared to full assembly.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

The 16 inch display is large with more surface area. We had one where the corner got bent from a fall. Full assembly was the only option but it came out looking brand new.

Liquid Retina XDR and ProMotion

The A2780 display uses mini-LED backlighting with ProMotion for 120Hz refresh. True Tone colour adjustment is included. The calibration data lives in the display housing. As such LCD only repair keeps True Tone working because that housing stays in place. Full assembly means new housing and True Tone data doesnt transfer across.

The team have worked on alot of these big displays. The XDR quality is excellent for professional work.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage is another one where something gets pressed against the closed lid. The mini-LED display can fail in sections showing dark patches or colour distortion.

Professional machines need proper repairs. Thats what we do.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 16" M2 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
