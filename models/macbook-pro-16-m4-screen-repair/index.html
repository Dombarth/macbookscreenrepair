MacBook Pro 16" M4 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 16 inch M4. The latest generation uses a Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion at 3456 x 2234 resolution. Available with M4 Pro or M4 Max chips. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The M4 16 inch is Apples newest large laptop released late 2024. Fourth generation Apple Silicon with incredible performance. Weve already begun working on these machines as repairs come in.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair approach. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

LCD only repair preserves your True Tone calibration since the housing with its calibration data stays in place. Full assembly means new housing and True Tone wont transfer.

Liquid Retina XDR and ProMotion

The M4 display uses mini-LED backlighting with ProMotion for 120Hz refresh. True Tone colour adjustment is included. The calibration data lives in the display housing. With LCD only repair True Tone keeps working. Full assembly and True Tone doesnt transfer.

The nano-texture glass option on some M4 models requires special handling. Let us know if you have this display type.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The large 16 inch display has significant surface area. Pressure damage where something presses against the closed lid. The mini-LED display can fail in sections showing dark patches or distortion. Notch area damage from impacts.

New machines still break. We can help when they do.

M4 Pro vs M4 Max

Both chip variants use compatible display assemblies in the 16 inch chassis. Whether you have M4 Pro or M4 Max doesnt affect the screen repair. The chip variant relates to performance not the display.

The Max has more GPU cores and higher memory bandwidth but display wise theyre the same.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 16" M4 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are available. Being a newer model part availability may vary. Get in touch to check current stock.
