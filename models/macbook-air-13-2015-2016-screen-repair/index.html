MacBook Air 13" 2015-2016 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2015 and 2016 models. Both years use the A1466 model number with a display at 1440 x 900. These are non-Retina screens and weve seen alot of them over the years. We do LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 2015-2016 Air was a popular machine. Solid build quality and people held onto them for years. Had one from Carlingford where the screen cracked after a fall from a couch. Nothing dramatic but enough to crack the LCD. Housing was fine so LCD only repair sorted it out.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel while keeping your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or warping for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly is the way forward.

Non-Retina Display

The A1466 from 2015-2016 uses a standard LCD panel at 1440 x 900 resolution. Lower pixel density than newer Retina models but still a functional display for everyday tasks. No True Tone on these machines so theres nothing extra to worry about during repair.

Common Issues

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. Pressure damage is another common one where something presses against the closed lid. On machines this age we also see wear related problems. Backlight issues, vertical lines, display cables wearing out from years of opening and closing.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Air 13" 2015-2016 screen repairs after seeing the machine. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation. Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock.
