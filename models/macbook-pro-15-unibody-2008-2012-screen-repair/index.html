MacBook Pro 15" Unibody 2008-2012 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch Unibody from 2008 to 2012. The A1286 was the pre-Retina 15 inch Pro with display at 1440 x 900. This design ran from late 2008 through mid 2012. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The Unibody design was revolutionary when it came out. Solid aluminium construction. Some of these machines are still running and people want them fixed. Had one from Parramatta where the owner used it as a backup machine. Screen cracked but housing was surprisingly good for its age. LCD only repair worked out.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

On machines this old we see alot of wear. Dents, worn hinges, damaged cables. We check everything and explain what makes sense for your situation.

Non-Retina Display

The A1286 uses a standard LCD at 1440 x 900 resolution. Not Retina. No True Tone or any fancy calibration to worry about. Simple display replacement when the housing allows.

Parts for these older machines can be harder to source but theyre still available. Worth repairing if you want to keep the machine going.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. Pressure damage from things pressing against the closed lid. On machines this age we see alot of other issues too. Backlight failure, vertical lines, display cables completely worn out, hinge problems.

Some of these are fifteen years old. Age related wear is expected.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" Unibody screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show the real condition. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Turnaround depends on parts availability for these older models. The team can give you a timeline when you bring it in.
