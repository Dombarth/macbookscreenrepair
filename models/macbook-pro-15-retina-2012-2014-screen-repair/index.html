MacBook Pro 15" Retina 2012-2014 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch Retina 2012 to 2014 models. The A1398 uses an IPS LED backlit Retina display at 2880 x 1800 resolution. These were the first 15 inch Retinas and established the high resolution standard. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The original 15 inch Retina was groundbreaking. 2880 x 1800 resolution was incredible at the time. Despite their age many are still in use. We repair them when it makes sense to do so.

Model Variations

The A1398 model number covers several production years. Mid 2012 was the original release. Late 2013 and Mid 2014 were updates with faster processors and improved graphics. The display panels are similar across these years with some variations in cable routing and connector placement. We have experience with all variations.

Check your serial number if youre unsure which year you have. We can help identify the exact model.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair approach. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

For machines this age the housing has often accumulated some wear. Well assess whether LCD only is viable or if full assembly is needed.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are still common. Pressure damage from items on closed lids. The coating delamination issue affects many units of this era showing as marks or stains that wont clean off. Vertical lines or display artefacts from aging components. General wear and degradation from years of use.

These machines have had a long life. Issues reflect that.

Worth Repairing?

Honest assessment is needed for machines this age. If the rest of the machine is healthy and meets your needs then repair can make sense. If youre experiencing other problems like battery failure, slow performance or other issues then replacement might be more practical. We can advise based on your specific situation.

Some people specifically want to keep older machines running. We respect that.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" Retina 2012-2014 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Parts availability for these models can vary. Well check stock when you get in touch.
