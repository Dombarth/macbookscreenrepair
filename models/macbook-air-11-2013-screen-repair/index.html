MacBook Air 11" 2013-2014 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 11 inch 2013 and 2014 models. Both use model identifier A1465 with compatible display assemblies. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on the housing condition.

The 2013 and 2014 11 inch Airs were the Haswell generation with improved battery life. Same compact design that made the 11 inch Air popular for travel and light use. 11.6 inch display at 1366 x 768 resolution.

Compact 11 inch Design

The 11 inch Air was Apples most portable MacBook at the time. Small and light enough to take anywhere. People used them for travel, writing, basic work. Apple discontinued the 11 inch but some people still have them and still need repairs.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. Full display replacement swaps the whole assembly.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal LCD panel while keeping the original display housing. Housing stays, camera stays, backlight stays. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends. No liquid exposure. Given the age of these machines the housings have often taken wear.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure. These mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new cables. Bigger repair but sometimes thats whats needed for these older machines.

Still Worth Repairing?

The 2013-2014 11 inch Airs are over ten years old. Whether repair makes sense depends on what else is going on with the machine. If the rest works fine and it does what you need then a screen repair can extend its life. We assess and give you pricing, you decide.

Drop Off Service

All MacBook Air 11" screen repairs are drop off only. Bring it in, we assess the damage and housing, work out the repair type and give you pricing.
