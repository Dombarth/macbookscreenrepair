MacBook Air 2017 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2017 model. This is model identifier A1466 which was the last of the non-retina MacBook Air generation. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2017 MacBook Air uses the classic Air design with the wider bezels and standard definition display. 13.3 inch screen running at 1440 x 900 resolution. Its not a Retina display like the newer models but plenty of people still use them and they still need repairs when screens get damaged.

Non-Retina Display

The 2017 Air has the older non-retina display at 1440 x 900 resolution. Thick silver bezels around the screen. MagSafe charging port rather than USB-C. If your Air has a MagSafe connector and wide bezels around the screen then its one of these older models.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly including the housing gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the 2017 Air means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing. The housing stays, camera stays, backlight and cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid damage around the display. Bezel intact. The team at MacBook Screen Repair assess all of this when you drop off.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure, cracked bezels. In these cases LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats what these older MacBooks need.

Common Issues

The 2017 Air has been around for a long time now so weve seen alot of them with screen problems. Cracked screens, dead displays, flickering, backlight issues. These machines are getting on in age but people still use them daily and accidents still happen.

Being older the housings have usually taken some wear over the years. This can affect whether LCD only is possible. We check each one at assessment.

A1466 Model Years

The A1466 model number covers several years of MacBook Air. 2012 through to 2017 all used A1466. The parts are generally compatible between them. If your not sure which year you have check About This Mac or bring it in.

Drop Off Service

All MacBook Air 2017 screen repairs are drop off only. Bring the MacBook in, we assess the damage and housing condition, work out whether LCD only or full display replacement is needed and give you pricing.

MacBook Air 2017 Questions

Can you still repair these older MacBook Airs?
Yes we still repair the 2017 and earlier MacBook Air models. Parts are still available for the A1466 displays.
Is the 2017 Air a Retina display?
No the 2017 has the older non-retina display at 1440 x 900. The first Retina Air was 2018.
How do I know what year my Air is?
Check About This Mac under the Apple menu. It shows the model name and year. Or bring it in and we can check.
