MacBook Pro 16" M4 Pro/Max Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Pro 16 inch M4 Pro and M4 Max released in 2024. This is model identifier A3083. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 16 inch MacBook Pro with M4 Pro or M4 Max chip has the largest Liquid Retina XDR display Apple makes. 16.2 inch panel running at 3456 x 2234 resolution with mini-LED backlighting and ProMotion. Popular with video editors and creative professionals who need the extra screen space.

Largest MacBook Display

The 16 inch display is massive for a laptop. Mini-LED backlighting with ProMotion for adaptive refresh up to 120Hz. HDR support with 1600 nits peak brightness. Same display technology as the 14 inch but bigger panel means alot more surface area.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel with mini-LED backlight array, camera module and cabling. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal display panel while still keeping your original housing. Metal frame stays, camera stays, cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents or bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team at MacBook Screen Repair assess all of this when you drop off.

LCD only is our preferred repair when conditions allow. The 16 inch panels are expensive so LCD only saves significant cost over full display replacement when the housing is eligible.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new display panel, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair and more expensive than LCD only.

Full display replacement can affect True Tone. Apple ties this feature to the original display that independent repairers cant restore.

Common Issues

We see cracked screens from drops and impacts on these machines. The larger size makes them more prone to accidents during transport. Dead displays, backlight issues, lines across the screen. These are expensive machines used by professionals who need them working.

All MacBook Pro 16" M4 screen repairs are drop off only. Bring the MacBook in, we assess the damage, determine the repair type and give you pricing before any work starts.
