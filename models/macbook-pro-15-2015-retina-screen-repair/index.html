MacBook Pro 15" 2015 Retina Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch 2015 Retina. The A1398 Mid-2015 uses an IPS LED backlit Retina display at 2880 x 1800 resolution. This was the last of the classic 15 inch Retina design before Touch Bar arrived. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 2015 15 inch remains popular. Excellent keyboard and good performance for its age. Weve repaired plenty of them over the years. Still worth fixing if the rest of the machine works well.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair approach. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

The A1398 display is well designed. The LCD panel can often be replaced separately which keeps costs down when the housing is in good shape.

Retina Display Quality

The 2015 15 inch Retina has a 2880 x 1800 IPS panel with excellent colour accuracy. LED backlit with wide viewing angles. At this age some displays develop coating issues or wear marks. We can help with those problems too.

The display lamination can degrade over time showing marks that look like stains. This is fixable with LCD replacement.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. The display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage where something presses against the closed lid. Coating delamination showing as marks or stains on the display surface. Vertical lines from cable wear.

The 2015 15 inch has proven durable. Many still in daily use.

Worth Repairing?

If your 2015 15 inch Pro still runs well then screen repair makes sense. The cost of repair is far less than buying a newer machine. Many people prefer the older keyboard design over the butterfly keys that followed. Consider your use case and well give you honest advice.

Parts availability for A1398 is still good.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" 2015 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability.
