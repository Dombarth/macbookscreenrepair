MacBook Air 2019 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2019 model. This is model identifier A1932, same as the 2018 model but with updated internals. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2019 MacBook Air was a refresh of the 2018 Retina Air with a True Tone display added. Same chassis design with the thinner bezels compared to the older non-retina Airs. The display is a 13.3 inch Retina panel running at 2560 x 1600 resolution. When screens get damaged we can replace either just the LCD panel or the complete display assembly.

About the 2019 Air

The 2019 Air uses the same model number A1932 as the 2018 model. Main difference is True Tone support which the 2018 didnt have. Both have the same display panel physically so repair parts are compatible between them.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera module and display cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel and keep everything else. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the 2019 Air means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing intact. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight and cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the display housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team at MacBook Screen Repair assess all of this when you drop off.

LCD only is what we prefer to do when conditions allow. Keeps your original housing and camera. True Tone usually stays working because the original electronics remain in place.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid got into the display area, cracked bezels. In these cases LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD panel, new camera, new backlight, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats what the MacBook needs.

Full display replacement can affect True Tone functionality. Apple ties this feature to the original display hardware that independent repairers cant restore.

Common Issues

The 2019 Air has been around for quite a few years now so weve seen alot of them with screen damage. Cracked screens from drops are most common. Dead displays, flickering, lines across the screen, backlight problems. These MacBooks have had plenty of time for accidents to happen.

Older machines often have more wear on the housing which can affect eligibility for LCD only repair. We assess each one individually when it comes in.

Same model number A1932 and same display design. Main difference is 2019 has True Tone support. Parts are compatible between them.
