MacBook Pro 14" M4 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Pro 14 inch M4 released in 2024. This is model identifier A3081 with the base M4 chip. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 14 inch MacBook Pro with M4 chip has the Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. 14.2 inch display running at 3024 x 1964 resolution with mini-LED backlighting. Beautiful display but expensive to replace when something goes wrong. When screens get damaged we can replace either just the LCD panel or the complete display assembly.

Liquid Retina XDR Display

The MacBook Pro 14" M4 uses the same display technology as the M3 and M2 Pro models. Mini-LED backlighting with ProMotion for adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. HDR support with 1600 nits peak brightness. The notch at the top houses the camera.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel with its mini-LED backlight array, camera module and display cabling. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly including housing gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the MacBook Pro 14" M4 means swapping just the internal display panel while still keeping your original display housing intact. Metal frame stays, camera stays, all the cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the display housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents or bends in the frame. No signs of liquid exposure around the display. Bezel and corners in good condition including the notch area. The team at MacBook Screen Repair check all of this at assessment.

LCD only is our preferred repair when the housing allows for it. More targeted than full display replacement. Keeps your original housing and camera. True Tone usually stays working because the original electronics remain.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement for the MacBook Pro 14" M4 is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display, damage around the notch area. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new display panel, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair than LCD only but sometimes thats what the MacBook needs.

Full display replacement can affect True Tone. Apple ties this feature to the original display through firmware pairing that independent repairers cant restore. Everything else works fine though.

Common Screen Issues

We see the usual range of screen issues on MacBook Pro 14" M4 models. Cracked screens from drops and impacts are common. These are expensive machines that people use daily for professional work so when accidents happen they need fixing quickly. Dead displays, backlight issues, lines across the screen.

The mini-LED display is complex technology. When the panel gets damaged it needs proper replacement with the right parts. We assess the damage and housing condition to determine the best repair approach.

MacBook Pro 14" M4 Questions

Can you repair a cracked MacBook Pro 14" M4 screen?
Yes we repair cracked screens on the MacBook Pro 14" M4. Either LCD only if the housing is undamaged or full display replacement if theres frame damage.
Will True Tone still work after repair?
LCD only replacement usually preserves True Tone. Full display replacement can affect True Tone because Apple ties this to the original display.
Is this the same as the M4 Pro model?
The base M4 model is A3081. The M4 Pro and M4 Max versions use model A3082. Different model numbers but similar display assembly.
