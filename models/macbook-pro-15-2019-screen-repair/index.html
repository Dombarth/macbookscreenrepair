MacBook Pro 15" 2019 Touch Bar Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch 2019 with Touch Bar. The A1990 uses an IPS LED backlit Retina display at 2880 x 1800 resolution with wide colour P3 gamut and True Tone. This was the last generation of the 15 inch before Apple moved to 16 inch. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 2019 15 inch was the final revision of this size. Strong performance and excellent display. Still very capable today. Weve repaired plenty of these machines for customers who want to keep them running.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair approach. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

LCD only repair preserves your True Tone calibration since the housing with its calibration data stays in place. Full assembly means new housing and True Tone wont transfer.

True Tone and Wide Colour

The 2019 15 inch has True Tone which adjusts white balance based on ambient lighting. The calibration data for True Tone is stored in the display housing. With LCD only repair True Tone keeps working. With full assembly replacement you get a new housing and the calibration doesnt transfer across.

The P3 wide colour gamut is excellent for creative work. Photo and video editors appreciate the colour accuracy.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage where something presses against the closed lid. Display flex cable issues can cause flickering or distortion. The 2019 model is recent enough that most issues are from physical damage rather than age.

Professional users rely on these machines. We understand the urgency when screens break.

Last of the 15 Inch

The 2019 15 inch was replaced by the 16 inch model later that year. If you prefer the slightly more compact 15 inch form factor keeping yours running makes sense. The display and performance are still competitive. Many people specifically chose this size over the larger 16.

No more 15 inch MacBook Pros will be made so repairs keep existing ones going.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" 2019 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability.
