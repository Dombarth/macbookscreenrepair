MacBook Screen Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we service screens across the MacBook Air and MacBook Pro range. Apple Silicon models with M1, M2, M3 and M4 chips. Intel models going back to 2008. LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement available depending on the condition of your MacBook.

We offer two repair types. LCD only replacement swaps just the internal panel when the housing is undamaged. Full display replacement is needed when theres frame damage or liquid exposure. Check our pricing guide for indicative costs by model. We service customers from Parramatta, Castle Hill, Blacktown and all across Western Sydney.

Understanding Repair Options

Not sure which repair type you need? LCD only replacement is our preferred option when the display housing is undamaged. It replaces just the internal panel while keeping your original frame, camera and backlight. Full display replacement is required when theres frame damage, liquid exposure or housing issues.

Concerned about True Tone? With LCD only replacement True Tone typically continues working. Full display replacement may affect this feature. We explain everything at assessment.

Dont see your model listed? We service most MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models. Get in touch to confirm we can repair your specific MacBook. Or book an assessment and bring it in.
