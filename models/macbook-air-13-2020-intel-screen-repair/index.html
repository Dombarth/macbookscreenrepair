MacBook Air 2020 Intel Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2020 Intel model. This is model identifier A2179. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2020 Intel MacBook Air was the last Intel Air that Apple released before switching to Apple Silicon. Same physical design as the 2018 and 2019 models but with updated internals. The display is a 13.3 inch Retina panel running at 2560 x 1600 resolution with True Tone support. When screens get damaged we can replace either just the LCD panel or the complete display assembly.

Last Intel MacBook Air

The 2020 Intel Air came out the same year as the M1 Air which replaced it. If you bought an Air in early 2020 you probably have this Intel model. Later in 2020 the M1 version took over. Both look identical from the outside so you need to check the model number to know which one you have.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera module and display cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel and keep everything else in place. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the 2020 Intel Air means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing intact. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight assembly and cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the display housing meets the eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No signs of liquid exposure. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team at MacBook Screen Repair check all of this at assessment.

LCD only is our preferred repair when conditions allow. More targeted than full display replacement. Keeps your original housing and camera. True Tone usually stays working because the original electronics remain.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure around the display, cracked bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD panel, new camera, new backlight, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats what the MacBook needs.

Full display replacement can affect True Tone functionality. Apple ties this to the original display through firmware pairing that independent repairers cant restore. Everything else works fine though.

Common Issues

Weve seen alot of 2020 Intel Airs come through with screen problems over the years. Cracked screens from drops are most common. Dead displays, flickering, lines across the screen. These MacBooks have been in use for several years now so theres been plenty of time for accidents to happen.

Because theyre older the housings have often taken some wear which can affect whether LCD only is possible. We assess each one when it comes in.

Intel vs M1

The 2020 Intel Air and 2020 M1 Air look identical from outside. Both have model numbers starting with A2. The Intel is A2179, the M1 is A2337. Check About This Mac to see which processor you have or bring it in and we can check for you.
