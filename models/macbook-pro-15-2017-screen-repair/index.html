MacBook Pro 15" 2017 Touch Bar Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch 2017 with Touch Bar. The A1707 uses an IPS LED backlit Retina display at 2880 x 1800 resolution with wide colour P3 gamut. This model shares the chassis design with the 2016 but has upgraded internals. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 2017 15 inch improved on the 2016 with faster processors and improved graphics. Same display panel though so repairs are handled the same way. Weve done plenty of these over the years.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair approach. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

The A1707 display assembly is well supported with good parts availability.

Wide Colour Display

The 2017 15 inch has the same display specs as the 2016. 2880 x 1800 IPS panel with P3 wide colour gamut and LED backlight. True Tone wasnt available on this model. Good colour accuracy for creative work.

The display technology was solid. Apple refined it further in later models.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. The display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage where something presses against the closed lid. Display cable flex causing flickering in some cases. At this age general wear issues can appear.

The 2017 model is old enough now that we see age related issues alongside accidental damage.

Worth Repairing?

The 2017 15 inch still handles many tasks well. If yours is in good overall condition then screen repair is worthwhile. Cost is much less than buying newer. Consider other factors like battery health and keyboard condition. We can give you an honest assessment of the whole machine.

Some people keep these running for a long time. Others are ready to upgrade.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" 2017 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability.
