MacBook Air 2013-2014 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2013 and 2014 models. These are the Haswell generation Airs using model identifier A1466. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2013 and 2014 MacBook Airs introduced the all day battery life that made them popular. Same classic Air design with MagSafe charging and the 1440 x 900 display. These machines are over ten years old now but some are still being used daily.

Haswell Generation

The 2013 Air was a big update internally with the Haswell processor that gave much better battery life. The 2014 was a speed bump with the same design. Both share parts with the 2015-2017 models since theyre all A1466.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera and cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing. Housing stays, camera stays, backlight stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends. No liquid exposure. Given the age of these machines the housings have often taken some wear. We assess each one at drop off.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure, worn bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes thats what these older machines need.

Still Worth Repairing?

The 2013 and 2014 Airs are getting old now. Whether its worth repairing depends on the condition of the rest of the machine and what you use it for. If the logic board and battery are still good and it does what you need then a screen repair can give it more years of life.

We dont make that decision for you. We assess the screen, give you pricing, and let you decide if it makes sense for your situation.

Drop Off Service

All MacBook Air 2013-2014 screen repairs are drop off only. Bring it in, we assess the damage and housing condition, work out the repair type and give you pricing.
