MacBook Pro 14" M2 Pro/Max Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Pro 14 inch M2 Pro and M2 Max released in early 2023. This is model identifier A2779. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 14 inch MacBook Pro with M2 Pro or M2 Max chip has the Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion. 14.2 inch panel at 3024 x 1964 resolution with mini-LED backlighting. Same display design as the M1 Pro models that came before.

Liquid Retina XDR Display

The M2 Pro and Max models use the same display technology as the M1 Pro generation. Mini-LED backlighting with ProMotion up to 120Hz. HDR support with 1600 nits peak brightness. Notch at the top for the camera.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel with mini-LED backlight, camera and cabling. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel. Full display replacement swaps the whole assembly including housing.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement means swapping just the internal display panel while still keeping your original housing. Metal frame stays, camera stays, cabling stays. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid exposure. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team assess this at drop off.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. As such its a bigger repair. True Tone may not work after full display replacement because Apple ties this to the original hardware.

Drop Off Service

All MacBook Pro 14" M2 Pro/Max screen repairs are drop off only. Bring it in, we assess the damage, determine repair type and give you pricing.
