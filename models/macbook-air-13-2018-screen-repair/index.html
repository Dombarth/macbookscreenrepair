MacBook Air 13" 2018 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2018. The A1932 was the first Retina MacBook Air and uses a display at 2560 x 1600 with True Tone. This was a major redesign from the older non-Retina models. We do LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 2018 Air was a big update when it came out. Retina screen, Touch ID, new design. Weve seen alot of these come through over the years. Had one from Kellyville where the owner dropped it getting out of their car. Screen cracked but housing was fine so LCD only repair sorted it out.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel while keeping your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly is the way to go.

True Tone

True Tone adjusts display colour based on room lighting. On the A1932 the calibration data lives in the display housing. As such LCD only repair keeps True Tone working because that housing stays in place. Full assembly replacement means new hardware and the True Tone data doesnt come across.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are the main thing. The Retina display fractures on impact with visible crack patterns. Pressure damage is another frequent one where something gets pressed against the closed lid. These machines are a few years old now so we also see age related issues.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Air 13" 2018 screen repairs after seeing the machine. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing is suitable. Assessment is free with no obligation. Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock.
