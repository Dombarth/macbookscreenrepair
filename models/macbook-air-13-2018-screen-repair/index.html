MacBook Air 2018 Screen Repair

At MacBook Screen Repair we specialise in the aspects of screen replacement for the MacBook Air 13 inch 2018 model. This is model identifier A1932 and was the first Retina MacBook Air that Apple released. The team provide LCD only replacement and full display assembly replacement depending on what condition the housing is in.

The 2018 MacBook Air was a complete redesign from the older non-retina Airs. New thinner bezels, Touch ID, USB-C ports, and most importantly a proper Retina display. The display is 13.3 inches running at 2560 x 1600 resolution. When screens get damaged we can replace either just the LCD panel or the complete display assembly.

First Retina Air

The 2018 Air was a big upgrade from the older A1466 models. Completely different display design with much thinner bezels. This was the start of the modern MacBook Air design that Apple has continued to refine with newer models.

Inside the display assembly theres the LCD panel, backlight, camera module and display cables. When we do LCD only repairs we swap just the panel and leave everything else. When full display replacement is needed the whole assembly gets replaced including the housing.

LCD Only Replacement

LCD only replacement for the 2018 Air means swapping just the internal LCD panel while still keeping your original display housing. Metal frame stays, camera stays, backlight and cabling stays in place. We remove the damaged panel and fit a new one.

This repair is available when the display housing meets eligibility requirements. No dents, no bends in the frame. No liquid exposure around the display. Bezel and corners in good condition. The team at MacBook Screen Repair check all of this at assessment.

LCD only is our preferred repair when conditions allow for it. Keeps your original housing and camera intact.

Full Display Replacement

Full display replacement is necessary when the housing has damage. Dented frame, bent corners, liquid exposure, cracked bezels. These situations mean LCD only wont work properly and full replacement is needed.

With full display replacement we swap the entire lid assembly. New housing, new LCD, new camera, new backlight, new cables. As such its a bigger repair but sometimes its what the MacBook needs.

Common Issues

The 2018 Air has been around for quite a few years now so weve seen alot of them come through. Cracked screens from drops, dead displays, flickering, lines across the screen. These older machines have had plenty of time for accidents.

Because theyre older the housings have often taken some wear and tear over the years which can affect whether LCD only is possible. We assess each one when it comes in.

2018 vs 2019

The 2018 and 2019 Air share the same model number A1932. Main difference is the 2019 added True Tone support. Display panels are physically the same so parts are compatible between them.
