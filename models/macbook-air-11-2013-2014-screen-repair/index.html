MacBook Air 11" 2013-2014 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Air 11 inch 2013 and 2014 models. Both years use the A1465 model number with a display at 1366 x 768. Compact machines that were popular for their portability. We do LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The 11 inch Air was a great travel machine. Small and light. Weve seen alot of them come through over the years. Had one from Penrith where the owner used it for work travel. Screen cracked in their bag. Housing was still in good shape so LCD only repair sorted it out.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending anything. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or warping for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly is required.

The 11 inch form factor means smaller components. We had one where the hinge was also worn from years of daily use. Did the screen repair and hinge replacement together.

The 11 inch Display

The A1465 uses a non-Retina LCD panel at 1366 x 768 resolution. Smaller than the 13 inch but suited the compact form factor. No True Tone on these models so theres nothing extra to worry about during repair. Straightforward job.

Parts for these older MacBooks are still available. Worth repairing if the machine still does what you need.

Common Issues

Impact cracks from drops are common. The display fractures on impact. Pressure damage is another one where something gets pressed against the closed lid. The LCD fails in patches but the glass can look fine from outside.

On machines over ten years old we see age related problems too. Backlight failing, vertical lines, display cables worn from years of opening and closing. All fixable with the right parts.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Air 11" 2013-2014 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition clearly. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no pressure.

Same day for LCD only when parts are available. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on stock. The team are happy to discuss timing when you get in touch.
