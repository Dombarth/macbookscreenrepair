MacBook Pro 15" Retina 2012-2015 Display Repairs

At Mac Screen Repair Sydney we specialise in the aspects of screen repair for the MacBook Pro 15 inch Retina from 2012 to 2015. The A1398 was the original Retina MacBook Pro with display at 2880 x 1800. This design ran from mid 2012 through mid 2015. We offer LCD only replacement and full display assembly depending on what is required.

The A1398 was the first Mac with Retina display. Revolutionary at the time and still decent screens. Weve seen alot of them over the years. Had one from Liverpool where the owner still used it for music production. Screen cracked after a studio mishap. Housing was ok so LCD only repair brought it back.

Repair Options

The team at Mac Screen Repair Sydney assess every machine before recommending a repair path. LCD only replacement means we swap out the damaged panel but keep your existing aluminium housing. The housing cant have dents or bends for this to work. If theres housing damage then full display assembly replacement is required.

On machines this age we see more housing wear than newer models. Dents from years of use, worn hinges. We assess everything and explain what your options are.

No True Tone

The A1398 predates True Tone so theres no calibration to preserve during repair. Makes things simpler in that regard. LCD only replacement is straightforward when the housing condition allows for it.

Parts for these older Retina displays are still available. Worth repairing if the machine does what you need.

Staingate Issue

Some A1398 models had a coating issue called staingate where the anti-reflective coating would degrade and look patchy. If youre seeing that and also have damage it might make sense to do LCD replacement anyway. The team can discuss options when you bring the machine in.

What We Usually See

Impact cracks from drops are common. Pressure damage where something pressed against the closed lid. On older machines we also see backlight failure, vertical lines, and display cables wearing out from years of use.

Some of these machines are over ten years old now. Age related issues are expected.

Pricing and Timeline

We quote MacBook Pro 15" Retina 2012-2015 screen repairs after seeing the machine. Photos dont always show housing condition accurately. LCD only costs less than full assembly when the housing qualifies. Assessment is free with no obligation.

Same day turnaround for LCD only when parts are in stock. Full assembly might take a day or two depending on availability. The team are happy to discuss specifics when you get in touch.
