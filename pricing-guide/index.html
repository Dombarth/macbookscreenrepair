Full Display Replacement

Full display assembly replacement is required when there is frame damage, liquid exposure or the housing is compromised. Includes new housing, panel, camera and all components.

Full display replacement costs more than LCD only. The entire assembly is replaced not just the panel. Pricing depends on model and parts availability. Expect 40-60% higher than LCD only prices listed above.

Full display replacement pricing varies significantly by model. The team provide an accurate quote at assessment once we confirm your MacBook requires full replacement rather than LCD only. We dont give full display quotes without seeing the machine first.

What Affects Pricing

Model and Screen Size

Larger screens cost more to replace. The 16 inch models have bigger panels than 13 inch models. Newer models with higher resolution displays or mini LED technology can have higher parts costs.

Repair Type Required

LCD only is less expensive because were replacing one component. Full display replacement costs more because youre getting the complete assembly. We determine which is needed at assessment.

Parts Availability

Common models usually have ready parts availability. Less common or very new models might require sourcing specific parts which can affect pricing and turnaround time.

Display Technology

Different display technologies have different costs. Standard LCD panels, mini LED panels, and varying resolution displays all have different parts pricing.
